Our Story

Connecting Faisalabad, One Business at a Time

Connect Faisalabad was born from a simple idea — every business in Faisalabad should be easy to find. Founded in 2024, our platform started as a small directory and quickly grew into the city's most comprehensive business discovery ecosystem.

We understand the challenges of finding the right service provider in a growing city. Whether you need a reliable software house, the best restaurant for a family dinner, or a trusted hospital — we bring every option to your fingertips.

Our team works tirelessly to verify each listing, ensuring you get accurate contact details, real reviews, and up-to-date information. We are powered by the community, for the community.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Our purpose and the future we are building for Faisalabad's business community

Our Mission

To create the most comprehensive, accurate, and accessible business directory for Faisalabad — empowering citizens to discover local businesses with confidence and helping businesses grow through digital visibility.

Our Vision

To become the definitive digital backbone of Faisalabad's economy — where every business, from a street-side café to a tech startup, has a verified presence and equal opportunity to connect with the community.

Our Values

We believe in transparency, accuracy, community empowerment, and innovation. Every feature we build is designed to bridge the gap between local businesses and the people who need them.

Our Impact

With thousands of businesses listed and millions of searches served, Connect Faisalabad has become the go-to platform for business discovery in the city, driving real economic connections every day.
