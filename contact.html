FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

How do I list my business?
Simply click the "Become a Partner" button in the navbar, fill in your business details, and submit. Our team will review and verify your listing within 24–48 hours.
Is listing my business free?
Yes! Basic listing is completely free. We also offer premium featured listings with additional benefits like priority placement, analytics, and more visibility.
How are businesses verified?
Our team manually reviews each listing, confirming contact details, address, and business information either via phone verification or official documentation.
Can I leave a review for a business?
Absolutely! Registered users can leave reviews and ratings for any business listing. We encourage honest feedback to help the community make informed decisions.
How do I update my business information?
Log in to your account, go to your business dashboard, and edit your listing details. Changes will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.
